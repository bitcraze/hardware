## Introduction

Use the Color LED deck, mounted on top of the Crazyflie, to create vivid lighting effects or provide visual feedback directly from the firmware. Its powerful WRGB LED offers full software control for color mixing, brightness, and behavior customization.

## Features

* One highly efficient high powered WRGB LED for maximum brightness facing upwards
* DC/DC driving circuitry for improved efficiency and consistent performance
* A light diffuser for smooth, even illumination and wide visibility
* Temperature protection
* Automatic board detection
* Compatible with the Crazyflie firmware to create custom patterns

## Electrical specification

* WRGB LED up to ~300mA per channel
* STM32C011 MCU as the LED controller

## Mechanical specifications

* Weight: 3.5g with the diffuser
* Size (WxHxD): 34.7x34.7x10.2mm with the diffuser
* Designed for mounting on top the Crazyflie facing upwards
