## Introduction

Attach the LED Diffuser to your Color LED deck or LED-ring deck to evenly spread and soften the light. It extends visibility to the sides while creating a smooth, even glow — perfect for improving visual feedback or adding a polished look to your drone’s illumination.

## Features

* Compatible with both versions of the Color LED deck and the LED-ring deck
* Optimized light and color diffusion for even, uniform illumination
* Designed for increased side visibility
* Snap-on mount for a secure fit
* Compact design that fits neatly under the Crazyflie
