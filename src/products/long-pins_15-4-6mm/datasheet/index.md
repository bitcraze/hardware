The Crazyflie<sup>TM</sup> long pins 15+4+6mm are designed to increase expansion capability by enabling additional decks to be mounted on the Crazyflie platform. The wider plastic spacer provides mechanical rigidity and extra support for the AI-deck when mounted underneath, helping keep it level.
{% enddatasheet_intro %}

## Introduction

The long deck pins are compatible with all products that have a deck expansion header.

## Electrical specification

* Current rating: 2 A max
* Voltage rating: AC 500V
* Insulation resistance: 1000MOhm min
* Contact resistance: 20mOhm max

## Mechanical specification

* Number of positions: 10
* Spacing: 2mm
* Pin thickness: 0.5mm
* Sections: pin 15mm - plastic 4mm - pin 6mm
* Weight: 0.7g
* Size (BxHxW): 20x25x2mm
