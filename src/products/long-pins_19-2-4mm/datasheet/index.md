The Crazyflie<sup>TM</sup> long pins 19+2+4mm are designed to increase expansion capability by enabling additional decks to be mounted on the Crazyflie platform. By default, they support the attachment of up to two decks on top and one deck on the bottom side of the Crazyflie.
{% enddatasheet_intro %}

## Introduction

The long deck pins are compatible with all products that have a deck expansion header.

## Electrical specification

* Current rating: 2 A max
* Voltage rating: AC 500V
* Insulation resistance: 1000MOhm min
* Contact resistance: 20mOhm max

## Mechanical specification

* Number of positions: 10
* Spacing: 2mm
* Pin thickness: 0.5mm
* Sections: pin 19mm - plastic 2mm - pin 4mm
* Weight: 0.6g
* Size (BxHxW): 25x20x2mm
