Crazyflie<sup>TM</sup> short pins are best suited for manual flight with only one deck mounted on top. The Crazyflie<sup>TM</sup> medium pins are designed for two decks total, one deck on top and one deck on the bottom of the Crazyflie.
{% enddatasheet_intro %}

## Introduction

This part is compatible with all products that have a deck expansion header.

## Electrical specification

* Current rating: 2 A max
* Voltage rating: AC 500V
* Insulation resistance: 1000MOhm min
* Contact resistance: 20mOhm max

## Mechanical specification

* Number of positions: 10
* Spacing: 2mm
* Pin thickness: 0.5mm
* Short pins
  * Sections: pin 9mm - plastic 2mm - pin 2mm
  * Weight: 0.4g
  * Size (BxHxW): 20x13x2mm
* Medium pins
  * Sections: pin 15mm - plastic 2mm - pin 2mm
  * Weight: 0.3g
  * Size (BxHxW): 20x19x2mm

## Package contents

* 4 x short pins
* 4 x medium pins
